StepUp Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Developer: Kameron Bains

App name: StepUP

1. Introduction

StepUp is a mobile fitness app designed to help users track their activity, set goals, and stay motivated through gamified fitness features.

This Privacy Policy explains how StepUp collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our app or related services.

By using StepUp, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

  • Your email address, name (from Google or Apple sign-in).
  • Your username .
  • Basic metadata such as your account creation date and progress stats.

b. Fitness Data

If you connect your Google Fit or Apple Health account, we may collect:

  • Step count
  • Active calories burned

This data is accessed only with your explicit permission and is used solely to display your progress within the app.

c. Device Information

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Crash logs and error reports (via Firebase Analytics/Crashlytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Display your fitness statistics and progress rings.
  • Calculate XP, levels, and in-app challenges.
  • Sync and back up your progress to the cloud.
  • Improve app functionality and performance.
  • Send optional notifications or reminders (if you grant permission).

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data may be shared only in these limited cases:

  • Firebase Services (Google Cloud) – for authentication, analytics, and storage.
  • Apple Health / Google Fit APIs – only to read your fitness data as authorized by you.
  • Legal Requirements – if required by law or court order.

Your fitness and personal data are never shared with advertisers or used for marketing.

5. Data Storage and Security

All personal data is stored securely using Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore, protected by Google’s managed encryption.

We implement industry-standard measures to safeguard your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Secure tokens for authentication
  • Limited access controls for developers

6. User Control and Data Deletion

  • You can disconnect Google Fit or Apple Health at any time from your device settings.
  • You can request account deletion and data removal by emailing us at kam2711@outlook.com.
  • Upon deletion, all associated fitness data and user records are permanently erased from our servers.

7. Third-Party Services

StepUp integrates with:

  • Google Fit
  • Apple HealthKit
  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore

These services may collect information independently under their own privacy policies.

8. Children’s Privacy

StepUp is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child under 18 has created an account, please contact us for immediate deletion.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any significant changes will be posted within the app or on our website. Your continued use of StepUp after updates indicates acceptance of the new terms.
